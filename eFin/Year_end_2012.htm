Subject: IMPORTANT: Year End Impacts on Research Projects

 

What:

To facilitate financial reporting for the university’s March 31, 2012 fiscal year end there is a requirement to charge projects for goods and services purchased up to this date.  At March 31 there were a number of charges in the midst of being processed – to properly reflect these charges in your projects for March 31, 2012 the charges will be temporarily posted to your project in March and then reversed in April.

 

Please note the total dollars available to spend for research projects will not be affected – this process only ensures that charges are posted in the March time period. Once the original invoice is charged to the project no further actions are required.  This process will not create any additional transaction approvals.

 

What you will see in eFIN:

 

·         A number of charges posted in the March period

·         Reversal of these charges in April

·         Permanent posting of the final charges in April through May
