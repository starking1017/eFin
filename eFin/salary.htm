SALARY ENCUMBRANCE CALCULATION - REVISED

(Effective October 19, 2009)

New Salary Encumbrance Calculation Methodology:

  1. Encumbrance based on the appointment end date.

  2. Prorated encumbrance calculations at employee and account levels.

  3. The current month’s wage encumbrance can not be displayed due to the current month’s actual wages posted on the 10th of the next month. The prior month’s wage encumbrance can be displayed. eg. Current month is March. End Date on eFIN has to be changed to February to show wage encumbrances.

  4. Encumbrances will reflect daily changes to project dates and appointment dates as they occur.

  5. Encumbrances which include recoveries for scholarships.

  6. New encumbrance calculation starts from April 1, 2007 to current. Anything prior to April 1, 2007 is using the old encumbrance calculation method.
